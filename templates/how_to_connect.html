How to connect

  1. Open Minecraft: Java Edition
  2. Click "Multiplayer"
  3. Click "Add Server"
  4. Enter "mc.digitalnook.net" into the "Server Address" box
  5. Click "done"
  6. Select the server from the list and then click "Join server"
